Summary:
The submission package from Summit Risk Advisors appears to contain material omissions
and potential misstatements that affected underwriting decisions.

Findings:
1. Prior loss omission:
   - Application stated no prior property losses in five years.
   - Inspection and maintenance interviews referenced claim CLM-2023-4410 at Plant 9.

2. Hazard profile omission:
   - Application stated no solvent storage above 50 gallons.
   - Inspection documented approximately 320 gallons of HexaSol solvent at Plant 14.

3. Financial misrepresentation risk:
   - Submitted revenue value: 42 million USD.
   - Audited financial packet reviewed post-loss: 79 million USD.

4. Coverage structuring concern:
   - Hazardous materials rider not included at bind.
   - Suppression control deficiencies were present before policy inception.

Recommended Actions:
- Open broker E&O reserve.
- Preserve all underwriting, broker, and insured communications.
- Coordinate with SIU regarding overlap with claim payment irregularities.
